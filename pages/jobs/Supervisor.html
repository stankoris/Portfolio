Supervisor of Housekeeping

Huma Kotor Bay Hotel & Villas

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Organization and planning of daily/weekly work schedules
  • Efficient team management
  • Guest communication at the hotel
  • Ordering office and other materials

At the start of the season, I worked as an assistant in housekeeping. After two months of work, the supervisor left, and I was given the opportunity to fill that position.

The job was challenging. I had a team of eleven people whom I needed to schedule daily and plan their daily activities. We were operating in a 5-star hotel, and there was no room for sloppy work.
Additionally, a small number of workers knew English, so I had to run around the hotel to communicate with guests every time they had a question.

Furthermore, I maintained the inventory and ordered necessary materials for the office and the rest of the team.
This job taught me a lot about organization, communication, and team management. I developed strong problem-solving skills and resourcefulness during this experience.
