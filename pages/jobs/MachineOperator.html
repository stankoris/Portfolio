Machine Operator

Amphenol d.o.o.

I worked two jobs here. I started as a machine operator and after a relatively short period, I advanced to a setter.

As an operator, my main responsibilities included:

  • Inspecting and ensuring product quality
  • Maintaining machines and keeping the area clean
  • Reporting errors and maintaining records of product quality and quantity

As a setter, my main duties were:

  • Operating the software for these machines
  • Adjusting the machines to achieve precise and accurate specifications (we're talking about millimeters)
    • I would receive instructions on how the product should look and my task was to set up the machine to produce such products. The machines produced conductors with millimeter-scale dimensions.
