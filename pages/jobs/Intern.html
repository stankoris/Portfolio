    After passing the test and the interview, I was notified that I had been accepted. Although I knew a challenging, sleepless period awaited me, I was happy and excited to have the opportunity to learn. And indeed, I learned a lot.

    We developed an application from scratch.
    We used MySQL for the database, MARS engine for the backend, and Vue.js for the frontend. Although I wasn't very familiar with JavaScript at the time, I quickly adapted.

    A period without rest followed. Despite being tired, my desire to learn kept growing.
    I realized everything I needed to know to apply theoretical knowledge in practice. After three exhausting months, along with two other students, I completed the internship and defended my thesis.
    And if you ask me, it was worth every sleepless night 😄
